12 That

Last time we learned two words for “this”:

  1. هَـٰذَا for boys or if the noun does not have ة.
  2. هَـٰذِهِ for girls or if the noun has ة.

Today, in-shaa-Allah we will learn how to say “that”:

  1. ذَ ٰلِكَ for boys or if the noun does not have ة.
  2. تِلْكَ for girls or if the noun has ة.

مَا ذَ ٰلِكَ؟ ذَ ٰلِكَ كَلْب
“What is that? That is a dog.”

مَا تِلْكَ؟ تِلْكَ شَجَرَة
“What is that? That is a tree.”

مَنْ ذَ ٰلِكَ؟ ذَ ٰلِكَ رَجُل
“Who is that? That is a man.”

مَنْ تِلْكَ؟ تِلْكَ بِنْت
“Who is that? That is a girl.”

From now on we will write هَذَا، هَذِهِ، ذَلِكَ instead of هَـٰذَا، هَـٰذِهِ، ذَ ٰلِكَ. But you must remember to read them with the alif.
